How this works:

Basically, you select what subject and set of flashcards you would like to use and then you select from a list of commonly banned sites or add your own. You change the amount of time you want to spend on the web. When you go to the banned sites beyond the allotted period of time, flashcards will pop up and you will have to answer them to keep browsing. If you answer all the cards correctly you are able to browse for as long as you like.
